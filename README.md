Bhetly is basically a modern digital profile platform where you can create one clean profile and share everything from a single link — your social media, portfolio, contact info, projects, business details, and more.

✨ Some cool features:
• One link for everything
• Clean and customizable profile
• Mobile friendly design
• Fast loading and modern UI
• Easy social/contact sharing
• Personal branding support
• Order Management
• Perfect for creators, students, freelancers & businesses

Instead of sending multiple usernames and links everywhere, you can just share your Bhetly profile.

I built this project with a lot of effort and ideas to make online identity sharing simpler and more professional. Would really love if you guys check it out and give me honest feedback ❤️
